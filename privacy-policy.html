Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Structr AI Private Limited ("Structr", "we", "our", or "us") and governs the collection, use, and sharing of personal information when you visit our website at www.structr.in or engage with our services.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a contact form, request an assessment, or communicate with us by email. This may include:

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity and improve your experience. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Contact form submissions are retained in our secure Google Workspace environment and deleted upon request.
