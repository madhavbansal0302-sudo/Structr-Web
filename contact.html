Common Questions

What to expect

How does the discovery call work?

It's a 30-minute video call. We ask questions about your business, your tools, and the problems you're running into. There's no agenda other than understanding your situation.

What happens after the call?

If there's a clear fit, we'll send a brief proposal outlining the scope, approach, timeline, and cost. No surprises — everything is agreed upfront.

What size businesses do you work with?

Typically small to mid-sized businesses (5–200 people) who are growing and finding that their operations aren't keeping up. Industry doesn't matter much — the problems tend to be universal.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Most clients choose a monthly retainer after the initial build — for monitoring, updates, and new automations as they come up. It's optional, never required.
